Kiawah Island Bobcats

This study consists of 60 Bobcats in Kiawah Island that have been captured and fitted with GPS collars. The Kiawah Conservation started this study in 2007 and is ongoing to the present day. Bobcats are a true inidication of a good Ecosystem. This Conservation study builds on land preservation of Kiawah Island and helps preserve the habitats of Bobcats. Research studies of conservation and preservation are good stewardship for a healthy environment for everyone. Should you wish to participate in this program, please Sign In.
